About Us

Chano restaurant was established in 2015 with a view of serving Nepalese cuisine. The term 'Chano' means roof in Nepalese language which implies that we accomodate our guests when they come to eat and give them the feeling of home. Our organic approach has resulted in zero food complains and the reviews from our wonderful customers speaks for itself. We ranked no. 1 in the "List of Best restaurant in Kathmandu" in the year 2020. This survey was conducted by NRA(Nepalese Restaurant Associations). Our team currently consists of 5 board members, 3 managers, 10 highly qualified chefs, 12 waiters and 2 assistants.
Our core objective is to serve nepalese food and drinks both to local and international customers. On top of that, We also aim to expand our resturant into several other locations around the country to create new employment opportunities for the youth.

We have a number of dishes that are a blend of amazing flavours that are lipsmacking.

Being bordered by India and China, the food is influnced by both indian , chinese and tibetian cuisins to some extent. We are ever expanding and have established our restaurants in other main cities of Nepal like Pokhara and Biratnager. In a years time from now in 2023, we aim to launch International cuisins like Italian , German and Danish.
